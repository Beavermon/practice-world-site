Frequently Asked Questions

What does the practice world do?

The practice world provides a way for all players to hone their skills. Modules include, hippo practice, blockin practice, wing practice and much more.

How long did this take to build?

2 Months.

How do I navigate the map?

Check out Oaics's showcase video. (Click Here)

Who built the map?

Building was split between Oaics and Beavermon.

How do I import the map?

Scroll up to the download button and click it. Once it has downloaded, open the file and the world will automatically be imported onto Minecraft. Then navigate to the 'singleplayer' section and the world should be there.

What versions does the world support?

As of v0.2, all versions above 1.20.30 are supported.

I found a bug, what should I do?

Please DM me or Oaics on Discord. Keep in mind that escaping the map is NOT a bug.

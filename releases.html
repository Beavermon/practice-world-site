Changelogs

v0.2

The CZB practice world has been updated, and it is so much better than the original!

What is new?

-Revamped Arrow Practice, arrow practice has been completely recreated with new Minecarts, Chicken, and Target bow practices

-Free Build, in addition to the diagonal bridge practice, there is now a free build mode.

-Movement practice, you can now practice your movement through a bypass with this new gamemode!

-Cybqr's block-in, in addition to oaics's block-in, we now have Cybqr's blockin world, a simplified version of the old one.

-NEW HUB, thanks to BeavermonE, we now have a beautiful new hub with plenty of secrets.

Furthermore, we have organized and made everything look more visually aesthetic!

-oaics & Beavermon

v0.1

This practice world has been long awaited, and now it is finally here! We are sincerely sorry that this took longer than it should've, with school, lack of motivation, and this was the first big command project for the creator.

What does this practice world have?

-Arrow Practice, with Moving Minecarts & Moving Targets.

-Bridging Practice, diagonal (free-build will be added soon).

-Block-In/Instamine Practice, with many different modes.

-Block Clutch Practice, suggested by dylpickle.

And most awaited of all, the Hippo Practice! This hippo practice includes Manor, Ender, and Lunary!

Almost all of these games are very customizable with their own timers and hotbars!
